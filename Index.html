Who is Rebecca Perla, you ask?

I am Rebecca Perla, most people call me Perla, and my pronouns are she/her/hers. I'm attending UW-Madison as a journalism, digital studies, and art history student.

I was born and raised in New York City, but I have lived in Canada, Israel, and Wisconsin as well. I'm a multi-disciplinary designer and writer with an emphasis in studio art and music journalism. When I'm not studying, you can find me roaming around a museum, listening to entrepreneur podcasts, or dancing at a concert. I live for music, fish tacos, and exploration. I have taken several reporting and graphic design courses and spend most of my time working on passion projects to hone my writing and artistic skills. If you want to see my portfolio that is dedicated to the arts, please visit: rebeccaperla.com
